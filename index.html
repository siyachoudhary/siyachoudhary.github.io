About Me

I am Siya Choudhary a high schooler, self-taught artist, and aspiring animator. Art found its way into me before I could barely form sentences. Every-time I lifted a crayon or pencil my mind wandered as I drew numerous drawings each with a story behind it. As I grew I began exploring the world of art even deeper and soon started working with various mediums.
Along with being an artist I am also a huge travel enthusiast and have visited just about 10 different countries across 3 different continents. I am also an avid sportsperson and love playing the violin as much as I relish different cuisines.
My goal is to pursue art and utilize my creativity to make the world a more colorful and happier place.
